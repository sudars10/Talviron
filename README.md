# Talviron

Talviron is an AI cardiometabolic coaching platform built to help people improve daily habits, medication adherence, and measurable metabolic outcomes.

## Vision
Build the most trusted daily coach for cardiometabolic improvement by combining:
- behavior change support for weight, nutrition, activity, sleep, and stress
- adherence support for GLP-1s, statins, and related care plans
- measurable trend tracking for weight, A1c-related risk, and cholesterol outcomes
- safety-first escalation when a user needs human support

## Product Direction
Talviron is being designed as:
- A member-facing daily coaching product that turns complex health goals into simple next actions
- An outcomes platform for employers, payers, and health partners that need engagement and cohort reporting
- A guardrailed AI system with auditability, escalation logic, and claim-safe boundaries

## What This Repository Is For
This repository is the home for Talviron product development and delivery.
