# Talviron

Talviron is an AI health coaching platform focused on helping people improve cardiometabolic outcomes through personalized, daily guidance.

## Vision
Build a trusted AI coach that helps users sustainably improve:
- Weight and lifestyle habits
- Prediabetes and metabolic risk factors
- Cholesterol-related outcomes
- Medication adherence behaviors

## Product Direction
Talviron is being designed as:
- A member-facing AI coaching experience
- A measurable outcomes platform for employers and health partners
- A safety-first system with clear escalation pathways

## What This Repository Is For
This repository is the home for Talviron product development and delivery.
